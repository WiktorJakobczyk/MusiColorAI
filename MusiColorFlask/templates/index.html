About Us

Each color affects human emotions differently, so it is important to choose the right colors when painting a picture. Our app allows you to experience the music created by colors.
The color palette's emotions are based on three scales:

  • Heat: Specifies the level of colors’ cheerfulness
  • Activity: Specifies the level of colors’ intensity
  • Weight: Defines the key of the generated music

We use 2 models to generate melodies that allow us to create both happy and sad soundtracks. In the future, we plan to create additional models that will allow us to generate different music genres.
